DormDishes Mac & cheese

A hearty, cheesy pasta. Perfect for filling the stomach of a hungry student. Freezer friendly!

Serves 4, 15 mins cooking time

Ingredients:

  • 220g macaroni pasta
  • 30g butter
  • 30g all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • Ground black pepper and salt to taste
  • 2 cups milk of your choice
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese

If you are a UK student, this cheddar cheese is highly reccomended!

Lets cook!

  1. Bring a pot of water to boil and cook the macaroni, stirring occasionally until al dente
  2. In a seperate pan, melt the butter over medium heat
  3. In the pan with the butter, add the flour, salt and pepper. Stir for around 5 minutes until smooth
  4. Pour in the milk slowly, while stirring continuously
  5. Cook for 5 minutes and keep stirring until mixture is smooth so the milk doesn't burn
  6. Add the cheese into the mixture and stir until melted
  7. Add the cooked pasta to the mixture and stir well
  8. Plate up and enjoy!
